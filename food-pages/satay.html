Veggie Satay Noodles

Love peanut butter? You'll love this 10 times more (and it only takes 30 mins to cook).

Ingredients

  • 3 peppers (not green)
  • 100g spring onions
  • 100g mangetout
  • 2 carrots
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • Thumb-size piece of ginger
  • Tin of coconut milk
  • Peanut butter (100% nuts & no palm oil!!)
  • Soy sauce
  • 250g egg noodles
  • 1 lime

Method

  • Slice all the veg into matchsticks and finely slice the garlic and ginger.
  • Add a glug of olive oil into a pan and add the veggies.
  • Cook until the veggies begin to char, then add the garlic and ginger. Cook for a couple more mins then set aside.
  • Cook noodles - check the pack for instructions.
  • Pour the coconut milk into a pan. Then add 1 tablespoon of soy sauce and 2 tablespoons of peanut butter. Stir and bring to the boil.
  • Add the juice of 1 lime and stir in. When the sauce is thick enough, add the noodles and veggies. Mix well and serve.
