Yasai Yakai Soba

Missing your cheeky wagas deliveroo? Or travelling around Asia where bowls of noodles are only 30 baht? This one's for you...

Ingredients

  • 2 carrots
  • 1/3 white cabbage
  • 1 white onion
  • 150g portobello mushrooms
  • 175g Shiitake mushrooms
  • 5 spring onions
  • Soy sauce
  • Mirin
  • Sesame oil
  • 400g wok ready noodles

Method

  • Finely chop the onion, shred the cabbage and chop the carrots into matchsticks.
  • Add two tablespoons of sesame oil and two tablespoons of vegetable oil to a pan. Add the onion, cabbage and carrots to the pan. Soften for 3 mins.
  • Chop the mushrooms. Add both types of mushrooms to the pan. Mix together and cook for 2 more mins.
  • Slice up 3 spring onions and add to the pan. Cook for 2 more mins. Add the noodles.
  • Season with pepper, add 6 tablespoons of soy sauce and 2 tablespoons of mirin.
  • Mix all together. Chop the 2 remaining spring onions for garnish whilst it cooks for 1 more min. Remove from the heat, garnish, and mmm!
